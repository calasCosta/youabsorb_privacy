YouAbsorb Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

📌 Summary: YouAbsorb is built with privacy by design. All your learning data stays in your browser. We have no servers, no accounts, and we never sell or share your information. You own everything.

1. What Data We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally (in your browser)

All the following data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using Chrome's storage.local API. This data never leaves your device.

  • Vocabulary words – Words you save, with definitions, examples, pronunciation, and your personal notes.
  • Mastery levels – How well you know each word (0-100%) and your review history.
  • Video history – List of YouTube videos you've analyzed, with titles, thumbnails, and timestamps.
  • Flashcard session results – Your practice activity, accuracy scores, and progress over time.
  • Settings – Your preferences and your OpenAI API key (if you choose to add it).

1.2 Data Accessed Temporarily (while you use the extension)

When you actively use YouAbsorb on YouTube, we temporarily access:

  • YouTube video metadata – Video ID, title, channel name, duration (to display in your history).
  • Video captions – To generate transcripts and extract vocabulary (captions are not stored permanently).
  • Your questions to the AI – When you use Chat, your questions are sent to OpenAI using your API key.

1.3 No Collection of Personal Information

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Browsing history outside YouTube
  • Passwords or financial information
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Analytics or usage statistics

2. How We Use Data

We use the data solely to provide and improve the extension's core functionality:

  • Vocabulary and flashcards – To help you learn and track your progress.
  • Video history – So you can easily revisit videos where you learned new words.
  • Chat and AI analysis – To answer your questions and extract vocabulary (using your own API key).
  • Transcript – To let you read along and click words to save them.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 OpenAI API (Optional)

If you choose to use the Chat or AI Analysis features, you must provide your own OpenAI API key. When you use these features:

  • Your questions and video context are sent to OpenAI's servers.
  • OpenAI processes this data according to its own privacy policy and API terms.
  • We never see or store your API key – it stays in your browser.
  • You control exactly when and how you use these features.

3.2 YouTube and Google APIs

We use YouTube's public API to fetch video metadata (titles, thumbnails) and captions. This is done in accordance with:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

3.3 No Other Third Parties

We do not integrate with any other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage method: All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage.local API.
  • Encryption: Your OpenAI API key is stored in your browser's local storage, which is accessible only to the extension on your device.
  • No cloud backup: We do not maintain any cloud servers or databases. If you uninstall the extension, all associated data is removed from your browser.

5. Your Control and Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View: All your vocabulary and history are visible in the extension's dashboard.
  • Edit: You can add notes to words, update your API key, or modify settings anytime.
  • Delete: You can delete individual words, videos, or your entire vocabulary from the dashboard.
  • Export/Backup: (Coming soon) – ability to export your data.
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension deletes all locally stored data from your browser.

6. Children's Privacy

YouAbsorb is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us (using the email below) and we will assist in deleting it from their browser.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Permissions Justification

YouAbsorb requests the following Chrome permissions, each strictly necessary for its functionality:

Permission Why Needed
tabs Detects YouTube navigation and reopens videos from your history.
storage Saves your vocabulary, video history, and settings locally in your browser.
*://*.youtube.com/* Displays the overlay on YouTube and reads video captions (only when you use the extension).
https://api.openai.com/* Optional Sends your questions to OpenAI only when you use Chat or AI Analysis with your own API key.
https://www.googleapis.com/* Fetches public video metadata (titles, thumbnails) for your history.
